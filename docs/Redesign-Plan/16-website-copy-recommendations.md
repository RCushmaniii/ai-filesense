## Privacy & Security

### Is my data sent to the cloud?
No. AI FileSense runs entirely on your computer. Your files are 
never uploaded, transmitted, or shared. The AI model runs locally.

### Do you collect any information about my files?
Never. We don't see file names, contents, or metadata. If you 
opt into anonymous analytics, we only collect aggregate usage 
patterns (like "how many users complete organization")‚Äînothing 
that could identify you or your documents.

---

## Organization

### What are the folder categories?
AI FileSense uses 11 smart folders:
- **Work** ‚Äî Employment, professional documents
- **Money** ‚Äî Financial, banking, taxes
- **Home** ‚Äî Household, utilities, property
- **Health** ‚Äî Medical, prescriptions, insurance
- **Legal** ‚Äî Contracts, agreements, licenses
- **School** ‚Äî Education, coursework, certificates
- **Family** ‚Äî Kids' documents, family records
- **Clients** ‚Äî Client-specific (for freelancers)
- **Projects** ‚Äî Project-based groupings
- **Archive** ‚Äî Older documents by year
- **Review** ‚Äî Low-confidence items for you to decide

### What goes in the Review folder?
Files where the AI isn't confident enough to categorize automatically.

and accurate.

### What's the difference between Simple, Timeline, and Smart Groups?
- **Simple**: Organizes by topic (Work, Money, Health, etc.)
- **Timeline**: Organizes by date (2024, 2023, etc.)
- **Smart Groups**: Groups by client, project, or detected entity

### Can I customize the folders?
The 11 main folders are fixed to ensure consistency, but subfolders 
are flexible. For example, Work can have subfolders for Clients, 
Projects, and Reports based on what the AI detects.

---

### Can I undo changes?
Yes. Every file operation is logged. You can:
- Undo a single file from the Activity Log
- Undo an entire organization session
- Undo even after closing and reopening the app

### What if the app crashes mid-organization?
When you reopen AI FileSense, you'll see a recovery dialog. You can:
- **Resume** where you left off
- **Undo all changes** made so far
- **Discard** and start fresh

### How long is the undo history kept?
Activity logs are retained for 90 days by default. You can export 
logs as text files for permanent records.

---

## Technical

### Does it work offline?
Yes. After installation, AI FileSense needs no internet connection. 
Everything runs locally on your computer.

### Does it work with Spanish documents?
Yes. AI FileSense detects Spanish text and uses Spanish keywords 
for classification. The app is fully localized for Mexico (es-MX).

### What file types are supported?
- PDF documents (.pdf)
- Word documents (.doc, .docx)
- Text files (.txt)

### What are the system requirements?
- Windows 10 (version 1903+) or Windows 11
